WELCOME TO JESSIE'S SPRINGS HOTEL

Welcome to Charleville Park Hotel, one of Cork’s finest four-star hotels. Recently awarded a 2022 Tripadvisor Travelers’ Choice Award, Charleville Park Hotel is proud to be named amongst the top 10% of hotels worldwide for delivering fantastic customer experiences.

Relax in the luxurious surroundings of the four star Charleville Park Hotel and Leisure Club on your next break away. Renowned for its friendly welcome, delicious food and relaxed atmosphere, there is no better place to escape for a night away or enjoy a family occasion than Charleville Park Hotel. Check out our latest promotions for your next break.

Our location in the heart of Munster allows you to explore the beauty of Cork and Limerick and the wonders of the Ballyhoura Countryside during your stay. We have created some fun sample itineraries to help you get the most out of your break away at The Charleville Park Hotel.
Boasting 91 bedrooms and suites, extensive leisure facilities, ample free car parking on site, and complimentary high speed WIFI, Charleville Park Hotel is your ideal base for your next getaway.

Our bedrooms and suites feature smart TV’s with Netflix, while our state of the art Leisure Club offers an extensive range of fitness classes. Enjoy our indoor 25 meter swimming pool while you are staying with us. Our spacious and comfortable Park Bar and Grill is the perfect places to catch up, relax and unwind.
An intimate and stylish lounge with adjoining courtyard garden is enjoyed in our Osprey Lounge, perfect for family occassions and private dining. Whether you are staying for business or leisure, Charleville Park Hotel & Leisure Club is the perfect choice for you. We look forward to welcoming you to Charleville soon.
